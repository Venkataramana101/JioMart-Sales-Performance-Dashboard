# 🛒 JioMart Sales Performance Dashboard

## 📌 Project Title / Headline
**JioMart Sales Performance Dashboard | Interactive Power BI Business Intelligence Report**

## 📖 Short Description / Purpose
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze JioMart's sales performance across executive, product, and regional perspectives. The dashboard provides actionable insights into sales revenue, product performance, delivery status, cancellations, and city-wise performance using dynamic KPIs, DAX measures, interactive bookmarks, slicers, and business storytelling.
---

- **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard Development & Visualization
- **Power Query** – Data Cleaning & Transformation
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – KPI & Business Measure Creation

- **Bookmarks & Buttons** – Interactive Navigation
- **Slicers** – Dynamic Filtering

## 📈 Business Impact & Insights

### Executive Insights
- Generated **₹1.77 Billion** in total sales revenue.
- Delivered orders contributed approximately **₹1.25 Billion**.
- Cancellation rate of **29.72%** highlights opportunities for operational improvement.
- Maharashtra emerged as the highest revenue-generating state.

### Product Insights
- Laptop category generated the highest revenue among all categories.
- MacBook Air emerged as the top-performing product.
- Realme Cable generated the lowest revenue.
- Premium electronic products contribute significantly to overall sales.

### Regional Insights
- Jaipur generated the highest city revenue.
- Kolkata ranked second in overall revenue.
- Delivered revenue consistently exceeded cancelled revenue across all cities.
- Top five cities contributed a significant share of total sales.
- Cities with higher cancelled revenue indicate opportunities to improve logistics and order fulfillment.

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 📄 Page 2 – Product Performance Analysis

### 📄 Page 3 – Regional Sales Insights
Focuses on city-wise revenue analysis, delivery performance, regional KPIs, state filtering, and key regional business insights to support strategic decision-making.
